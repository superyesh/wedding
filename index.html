A Tale of 2 States
Dec 2013
Starring Yeshwanth and Deepti

A long time ago, in a galaxy far,
far away in Long Island....

It is a period of new beginnings.

Fall colors, against a fear of impending
doom of Grad Life,
a simple boy from Chennai meets a
sweet girl from Pune.

During the great battle of graduate life,
they became best friends amidst
long walks and innumerable cups of java.

Pursued by the Master's sinister
assignments, the boy and the girl race
through their lives powered by
the Quantum Love Drive.

As Fall turns to Spring, Friendship
blossomed to Love.
A flower once opened that never closes. 

Join them for their epic journey... :P
Dec 9th @ Chennai 6pm Geetha Bhavan
